## 1. Account & Login

### I forgot my password. What now?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox (also spam)
5. Click the reset link in the email
6. Set a new password

> The reset link is valid for 24 hours.

### I am not receiving the reset email

**Check the following:**
- Is the email address spelled correctly?
- Look in your spam/junk folder
- Wait 5 minutes before trying again
- Contact your administrator if the problem persists

### How do I change my password?

1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your name (top right) > **Profile**
3. Select **"Change Password"**
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter the new password (twice)
6. Click **Save**

### What are the password requirements?

Your password must meet the following criteria:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least 1 number (0-9)
- At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)

### How do I change my language setting?

1. Click on your name (top right) > **Profile**
2. Find the **"Language Preference"** field
3. Select your preferred language:
   - NL Dutch
   - GB English
   - DE German
   - PL Polish
4. Click **Save**
5. The interface updates immediately

### Can I log in on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in on multiple devices simultaneously. Your session remains active for 24 hours. After 24 hours, you will be automatically logged out and must log in again.

## 2. Time Registration

### How do I clock in?

**Quick method (Dashboard):**
1. Go to the Dashboard
2. Click the large **"Clock In"** button
3. Allow location access if prompted
4. You are clocked in!

**Manual method:**
1. Go to **Time Registration**
2. Click **"+ New Entry"**
3. Fill in date, time, and break
4. Click **Save**

### Why does the app ask for my location?

GPS verification is used to:
- Ensure accuracy of time registration
- Confirm you are at the correct work location
- Prevent fraud

> **Privacy**: Your location is only recorded when clocking in and out, not continuously.

### I forgot to clock out. What now?

1. Go to **Time Registration**
2. Find the open entry (without end time)
3. Click **Edit**
4. Enter the correct end time
5. Click **Save**

> **Note**: This only works if the entry has not yet been approved.

### Can I enter hours retroactively?

Yes, through manual entry:
1. Go to **Time Registration** > **+ New Entry**
2. Select the correct date (in the past)
3. Fill in the details
4. Submit for approval

### How do I edit a time entry?

1. Go to **Time Registration**
2. Click on the entry you want to edit
3. Click **Edit**
4. Adjust the details
5. Click **Save**

> **Note**: Only entries with status "Pending" can be edited.

### What do the statuses mean?

| Status | Meaning | Action Possible |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Pending** | Awaiting approval | Edit/delete |
| **Approved** | Approved | No changes |
| **Rejected** | Declined | Resubmit |

### How do I export my hours?

1. Go to **Time Registration**
2. Set the desired filters (date, status)
3. Click **Export**
4. Choose the format: PDF, Excel, or CSV
5. Download the file

## 3. Leave Management

### How many vacation days do I have?

View your current balance:
1. Go to **Leave**
2. At the top, you will see your balance per category:
   - Statutory leave
   - Extra-statutory leave
   - Compensation hours

### What is the difference between statutory and extra-statutory leave?

| Type | Description | Default | Expiry Date |
|------|-------------|---------|-------------|
| **Statutory** | Minimum according to law | 20 days | July 1st next year |
| **Extra-statutory** | Additional days from employer | 5 days | 5 years after accrual |

### How do I request leave?

1. Go to **Leave**
2. Click **"+ New Request"**
3. Select the leave type
4. Choose start and end date
5. Optionally add a reason
6. Click **Submit**

### Can I cancel a leave request?

**Yes, if status is "Pending":**
1. Go to **Leave**
2. Find your request
3. Click **Cancel**
4. Confirm

**No, if status is "Approved":**
Contact your manager for cancellation.

### My leave was rejected. What now?

1. Review the reason for rejection
2. Discuss with your manager if needed
3. Submit a new request with adjusted dates

### When does my leave expire?

| Leave Type | Expiry Date |
|------------|-------------|
| Statutory leave | July 1st of the following year |
| Extra-statutory leave | 5 years after accrual |
| Compensation hours | December 31st of the current year |

> You will receive automatic reminders when leave is about to expire.

### What are compensation hours (TOIL)?

Time Off In Lieu: if you work overtime, you can save those hours and take them as leave later.

## 4. Sick Leave

### How do I report sick?

1. Go to **Sick Leave**
2. Click **"+ Report Sick"**
3. Select the start date (usually today)
4. Optionally add notes
5. Click **Submit**

> **Tip**: Report as early as possible, preferably before your work start time.

### How do I report recovery?

1. Go to **Sick Leave**
2. Find your active sick leave entry
3. Click **"Report Recovered"**
4. Enter the recovery date
5. Click **Confirm**

### What if I am returning to work part-time?

For partial return to work:
1. Open your active sick leave entry
2. Click **"Partial Recovery"**
3. Enter the percentage (e.g., 50%)
4. Describe any limitations
5. Click **Save**

### What is the UWV 42-day rule?

After 42 days (6 weeks) of illness, your employer must register you with the UWV (Dutch Employee Insurance Agency). ADSPersoneelapp automatically monitors this and sends timely alerts:

| Alert | Days Until Deadline |
|-------|---------------------|
| Warning | 7 days |
| Urgent | 3 days |
| Critical | 1 day |

### Who can see my sick leave?

- **You**: Your own sick leave records
- **Your manager**: Team sick leave (without medical details)
- **HR/Admin**: Absence overviews (without medical details)

> Medical information is never shared without your consent.

## 5. Expenses

### What expense types are available?

| Type | Description | Reimbursement |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Mileage** | Business trips | EUR 0.23/km |
| **Travel costs** | Public transport, taxi, parking | Actual costs |
| **Meals** | Business lunch/dinner | Max EUR 50/day |
| **Accommodation** | Hotel, lodging | Max EUR 150/night |
| **Other** | Other business expenses | On approval |

### How do I submit a mileage claim?

1. Go to **Expenses** > **+ New Expense**
2. Select **Mileage**
3. Fill in: date, from, to, distance, purpose
4. Upload parking receipt if applicable
5. Click **Submit**

### Do I always need to upload a receipt?

- **Mileage claims**: Not required, but parking receipts are needed
- **Other expenses**: Yes, always upload a receipt

### What file formats are accepted?

- **Images**: JPG, PNG (max 5MB)
- **Documents**: PDF (max 5MB)

### My receipt is unreadable. What now?

1. Take a new photo with better lighting
2. Ensure date and amount are visible
3. Upload the new receipt with the expense
4. Or: add an explanation of why the receipt is unreadable

### When will my expense be reimbursed?

Approved expenses are processed in the next payroll run (usually around the 25th of the month).

### My expense was rejected. What now?

1. Review the rejection reason
2. Correct the error (wrong amount, missing receipt, etc.)
3. Resubmit with the correct information

## 6. Fleet Tracking

### What is Fleet Tracking?

Fleet Tracking automatically links the GPS data from your company vehicle to your time registration, so:
- Trips are automatically logged
- Kilometers are accurately recorded
- Expense claims become easier

### How do I link my vehicle?

Contact your administrator. They can:
1. Link a vehicle to your account
2. Configure the Fleet Provider
3. Activate synchronization

### How do I view my trips?

1. Go to **Trips**
2. Select the date range
3. View your trips with details:
   - Start/end location
   - Distance (km)
   - Duration
   - Status

### How do I create an expense claim from a trip?

1. Go to **Trips**
2. Select the desired trip(s)
3. Click **"Claim"**
4. Kilometers are automatically filled in
5. Review and submit

### My trip is not being linked to my timesheet

This can happen if:
- The times do not overlap
- The vehicle is not correctly assigned
- Synchronization is delayed

**Solution**: Link the trip manually via the Trips page.

## 7. Managers & Approvals

### How do I approve items?

1. Go to **Approvals**
2. Review pending items by category
3. Click on an item for details
4. Click **Approve** or **Reject**
5. Optionally add a comment

### Can I approve multiple items at once?

Yes, batch approval:
1. Check multiple items
2. Click **"Approve All"** or **"Reject All"**
3. Confirm

### I am receiving too many approval requests

**Tips:**
- Set daily reminders (instead of per item)
- Use batch approval
- Delegate to a second manager (if possible)

### How do I add a new employee?

1. Go to **Employees** > **+ New Employee**
2. Fill in the basic details (name, email, position)
3. Select the role (User/Manager)
4. Choose the language preference
5. Click **Create**
6. The employee automatically receives a welcome email

### How do I deactivate an employee?

1. Go to **Employees**
2. Select the employee
3. Click **Deactivate**
4. Confirm
5. The employee can no longer log in

> Data is retained according to retention periods (GDPR).

## 8. Technical & Security

### Which browsers are supported?

| Browser | Version |
|---------|---------|
| Google Chrome | Last 2 versions |
| Mozilla Firefox | Last 2 versions |
| Safari | Last 2 versions |
| Microsoft Edge | Last 2 versions |

> Internet Explorer is **not** supported.

### Does the app work on mobile?

Yes! ADSPersoneelapp is fully responsive and works on:
- Smartphones (iOS and Android)
- Tablets
- Laptops
- Desktops

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously:
- **AES-256 encryption** for sensitive data
- **HTTPS** for all communication
- **Bcrypt** password hashing
- **GDPR** compliant
- **Audit logging** of all actions
- **Data in EU** (Supabase Frankfurt)

### How long is my data retained?

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Personnel files | 2 years after leaving |
| Salary data | 7 years |
| Absence data | 2 years |
| Audit logs | 7 years |

### Can I export my data (GDPR)?

Yes, you have the right to data portability:
1. Go to **Profile** > **Privacy**
2. Click **"Export My Data"**
3. You will receive a download link via email

### There is an outage. What now?

1. Check our status page: `status.adspersoneelapp.nl`
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact support if the problem persists
