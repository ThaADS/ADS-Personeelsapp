Getting Started

How do I create an account? expand_more
To create an account, download the AADS Personnel App from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and tap on the "Sign Up" button. You will be prompted to enter your service number, email address, and a secure password. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process.
Is the app available for all devices? expand_more
Yes, the AADS Personnel App is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download it from the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store respectively. Ensure your device meets the minimum operating system requirements listed on the store page.

Account & Profile

How do I reset my password? expand_more
If you've forgotten your password, tap the "Forgot Password?" link on the login screen. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you a link to reset your password.
Where can I update my contact information? expand_more
You can update your contact information by navigating to the 'Profile' section in the app menu. From there, select 'Edit Profile' to change your phone number, address, or other personal details. Remember to save your changes.

Using App Features

How do I view my schedule? expand_more
Your schedule can be accessed from the main dashboard or by tapping the 'Schedule' icon in the bottom navigation bar. You can view your upcoming shifts, request time off, and see your weekly or monthly calendar.
How can I submit a leave request? expand_more
To submit a leave request, go to the 'Schedule' section and tap the 'Request Time Off' button. Select the dates you wish to request off, choose the type of leave (e.g., vacation, sick leave), and add any necessary comments before submitting it for approval.

Troubleshooting

The app is not loading. What should I do? expand_more
First, check your internet connection. If the connection is stable, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, try restarting your device. As a final step, you can try reinstalling the app, but ensure any important data is backed up. If you still face problems, please contact support.
