How do I submit an expense report?

expand_more

To submit an expense report, navigate to the 'Expenses' section in the main menu, tap on 'New Report', fill in the required details, attach your receipts, and then tap 'Submit'.

What is the company policy on remote work?

expand_more

Our company supports a flexible remote work policy. Specific arrangements can be discussed with your direct manager and are subject to departmental needs and individual performance.

Where can I find my payslips?

expand_more

You can access all your payslips directly through the 'Payroll' section of this app. They are available in PDF format for download or viewing at any time.

How are vacation days calculated?

expand_more

Vacation days are accrued based on your length of service with the company. Please refer to the 'Leave Policy' document available in the 'Documents' section for a detailed breakdown of accrual rates.

How do I update my personal information?

expand_more

You can update your personal information, such as address or contact details, by navigating to 'My Profile' and selecting the 'Edit' option.
